ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE?

Who are we? G13 Car Rentals and Tours is a legitimately registered
travel and hospitality management company licensed by the Registrar
Generals Department of Ghana. We started operations in 2004 with
only two cars and by God’s grace, diligence, devotion, perseverance
and hard work we’ve been able to meet all standards in the car
rental and tourism industry, and we have a range of cars to suit all
occasions.
Presently our company is one of the leading tour organizations in
Ghana that provide transportation, accommodation and travel
arrangements for delegates to international conferences, seminars,
and workshops for individuals and corporate organizations.
G13 Car Rentals and Tours offers a highly comprehensive line up
of vehicles, customers can be confident of finding tailor-made
solutions to meet their need and budget.
G13 Car Rentals and Tours offers a wide range of cars available
for daily – weekly – monthly and yearly rentals; starting from
standard cars, sedans, SUVs, 4×4, Luxury cars, Luxury buses, and
commercial vehicles
